# TWGT

> Engineering Intelligent Infrastructure

TWGT is the engineering-led foundation for a modular ecosystem of intelligent applications, AI services, digital infrastructure, and knowledge systems.

Our mission is to transform engineering knowledge into reusable digital assets that support sustainable infrastructure, autonomous workflows, and long-term community value.

---

# Vision

Build an open, extensible platform where knowledge, software, and engineering converge to create intelligent systems that continuously learn, adapt, and improve.
